DRIVER SAFETY
Driver Safety FAQ

01 What safety features are included in the driver app?

The driver app includes key safety tools such as an in-app Emergency Assist button, dedicated support, rider management, driving time limits, location sharing, and women-only ride options depending on the country.

02 What vehicle requirements are needed to drive?

Vehicle requirements vary by city, but generally include a roadworthy car, valid registration, insurance, and compliance with local transport regulations.

03 How are driver ratings calculated?

Ratings are based on rider feedback after each trip and reflect overall driving quality, professionalism, and compliance with platform guidelines.

04 What happens if I’m involved in an accident?

In case of an accident, drivers can report the incident through the app. Our support and safety teams will guide you through the next steps and provide assistance.

05 How does the driver screening process work?

All drivers go through identity verification, document checks, and background screening before being approved to drive on the platform.

06 Is there a way to earn bonuses?

Yes, drivers may receive bonuses and incentives based on demand, completed trips, and special campaigns available in their city.

07 Where can I raise concerns or report issues?

Drivers can contact support directly through the app or visit a local hub to raise concerns and get help from our support team.
