POPULAR QUESTIONS
Frequently Asked Questions

01 How do I get paid?

Our payment cycle runs from Monday 00:00 to the following Sunday at 23:59. If you’re due a payout, we'll send it to you on a weekly basis — no need to wait until the end of the month.

02 How much does a delivery driver earn with Bolt Food?

Earnings depend on the number of deliveries, distance, time of day, and bonuses. Dynamic pricing during busy hours can increase your total earnings.

03 How can I find out how much I earned?

You can check your earnings directly in the Bolt Driver app under the "Earnings" section, updated in real-time after each delivery.

04 Will I deliver on a schedule, or can I choose my own hours?

You can choose your own hours. The platform is fully flexible, allowing you to deliver whenever it suits your schedule.

05 What are the requirements to sign up for a courier account with Bolt Food?

You need a valid ID, a smartphone (Android 9.0+ or iOS 12+), and a scooter, bike, or car with a valid license and insurance.
