POPULAR QUESTIONS
Frequently Asked Questions

01 What is Bolt Market?

Bolt Market is an online grocery delivery service inside the Bolt Food app which allows you to order groceries and have them delivered straight to your door.

02 How does Bolt Market work?

Simply download the Bolt Food app, select a nearby Bolt Market store, add your groceries to the basket, confirm and pay, and our team will deliver your order directly to your door.

03 Is Bolt Market available in my area?

Bolt Market availability depends on your city. Open the Bolt Food app and check if Bolt Market stores are near your location.

04 What types of delivery products can I order?

You can order fresh produce, pantry staples, household essentials, beverages, snacks, and more — all available in the Bolt Market section of the app.

05 How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered within 30 minutes, depending on store availability and your location.

06 How can I track my delivery?

You can track your order in real-time within the Bolt Food app, from the store shelf to your doorstep.

07 Does Bolt Market offer same-day delivery?

Yes! All Bolt Market orders are delivered the same day, usually within 30 minutes.
