ABOUT Taxif
The First European Mobility Super-App

Taxif is transforming urban mobility. We fight for better cities and against private cars, offering a smarter alternative for ride-hailing, shared cars and scooters, and food & grocery delivery. Our mission is to make transport easier, greener, and more accessible for everyone.

  • Founded in 2013, revolutionizing urban mobility across Europe
  • Serving over 200+ million customers worldwide
  • More than 4,500,000 drivers and delivery partners

Founded by Markus Villig in 2013, Taxif serves over 200+ million customers across 50+ countries. We are committed to greener urban mobility, reducing carbon emissions through supporting sustainable projects worldwide.
