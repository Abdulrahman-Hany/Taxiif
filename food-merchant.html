POPULAR QUESTIONS
Frequently Asked Questions

01 What is Bolt Food?

Bolt Food is a delivery solution for restaurants and merchants. The Bolt Food app connects partner restaurants and merchants with millions of customers who want to order food, groceries, and more.

02 How can I become a Bolt Food merchant?

You can sign up directly via the Bolt Food merchant portal. Provide your business details and complete the registration process to start receiving orders.

03 Are there any specific requirements to become a Bolt Food merchant?

Requirements vary by country, but generally you need a valid business license and comply with local food safety and delivery regulations.

04 Does Bolt Food only work with restaurants, or can other businesses also join?

Bolt Food supports restaurants, grocery stores, and other eligible merchants depending on your country. Contact Bolt Food to check eligibility for your business.

05 How does Bolt Food help merchants increase their revenue?

Bolt Food increases visibility to millions of customers, offers marketing support, and provides tools for efficient order management to help merchants grow their sales.
