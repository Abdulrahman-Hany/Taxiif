POPULAR QUESTIONS
Frequently Asked Questions

01 What are the requirements to use Bolt Drive?

To use Bolt Drive, you need a valid driving licence, a verified Bolt account, and a payment method added to your profile. Some countries also require a minimum age or driving experience—check the app for local details.

02 What kind of cars can I rent with Bolt Drive?

Bolt Drive offers a wide variety of vehicles, including everyday cars, SUVs, vans, trucks, and luxury cars. Availability depends on your location—check the app for cars near you.

03 Can I do intercity trips with Bolt Drive cars?

Intercity trips are allowed depending on the car type and local regulations. Always check the app and rental terms before starting your trip.

04 How do I find a Bolt Drive car?

You can locate cars using the in-app map. Cars on the street are available instantly, or you can reserve one ahead of time if available.

05 How does reserving a car work?

Open the app, select a car, and choose the duration. Once confirmed, the car is reserved for you for the specified time.

06 How do I pay for Bolt Drive?

Payments are processed through the app. You can pay for the time and distance you drive using the available payment methods in your region.
