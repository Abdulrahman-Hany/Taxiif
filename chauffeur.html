FAQ
Answers to questions on luxury cars, chauffeurs, and how to reserve

01 How do I reserve a journey?

Taxif journeys can be reserved through the Ride Booker tool on Taxif’s website or in the Taxif Business portal. You can also reserve cars directly in the Taxif app (availability dependent on your region).

02 What is a concierge?

A concierge is a dedicated professional who assists you with planning and managing all aspects of your Taxif journeys, ensuring a seamless travel experience.

03 What is your cancellation policy?

Taxif allows flexible cancellation depending on your booking type. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific terms.

04 What if my flight is delayed or arrives early?

Taxif monitors flight schedules and adjusts your chauffeur pickup accordingly to ensure timely service regardless of delays or early arrivals.

05 Can I make changes during my journey?

Yes, you can modify your journey in real-time via the Taxif app or by contacting your concierge.

06 What is the luggage capacity for each service class?

Luggage capacity varies by service class. Business Class accommodates standard luggage for 2 passengers, First Class for 3-4 passengers, and XL Class for larger groups with extra luggage.

07 Is pricing all-inclusive?

Yes, pricing includes chauffeur service, vehicle, and any applicable fees. Additional services may incur extra charges.
