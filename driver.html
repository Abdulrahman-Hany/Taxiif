HOW IT WORKS

Reliable and easy to use, the Taxif Driver app gives you everything you need to drive, earn money, and stay in full control of your schedule.

Accept a ride request

Pick up your passenger

Drive to the destination

Repeat & earn more

Keep driving and earning or go offline anytime — you’re always in control.

FAQ
Frequently asked questions from drivers

Be your own boss. Start driving and earning with Taxif. It takes just 2 minutes to submit your information.

Apply online

01 Can I drive with Taxif in my city?

Taxif is available in hundreds of cities worldwide. Check our cities list to see if you can start driving in your area.

02 How do I register to drive with Taxif?

Simply apply online, choose your city, upload the required documents, and follow the onboarding steps sent to your email.

03 Do I need a car to drive with Taxif?

No problem if you don’t have a car. Taxif can connect you with fleet partners who offer vehicles for rent.

04 Do I need a smartphone to drive with Taxif?

Yes, you need a smartphone to use the Taxif Driver app, accept ride requests, and navigate to passengers.

05 Is driving with Taxif a good side hustle?

Yes, you can drive part-time or full-time and earn whenever it fits your schedule.

06 How do I get paid for driving with Taxif?

You’ll receive weekly payouts directly to your account, with transparent commission and no hidden fees.
