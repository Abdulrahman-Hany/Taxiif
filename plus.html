POPULAR QUESTIONS
Frequently Asked Questions

01 How do I get the latest Taxif Plus benefits?

These benefits are subject to change as we always look to improve the service. Please make sure you have the newest version of the Taxif app in order to access the latest benefits. We’ll inform you of any significant changes to the benefits offered.

02 How does Taxif Plus work?

Taxif Plus gives you priority rides, discounted deliveries, free cancellations, and price protection for your trips and food orders.

03 How do I cancel Taxif Plus?

You can cancel your Taxif Plus membership anytime from the Taxif app. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.

04 How do I renew my Taxif Plus membership?

Your Taxif Plus membership will automatically renew unless you cancel it before the next billing cycle.

05 Where can I use Taxif Plus?

Taxif Plus can be used in all cities and regions where Taxif and Taxif Food services are available.

06 Do drivers still get paid the full amount for Taxif Plus rides?

Yes, drivers still receive the full fare for Taxif Plus rides, just like any other regular ride.

07 How does priority pick up work?

Priority pick-up ensures that drivers assigned to Taxif Plus users pick you up faster than regular rides.
