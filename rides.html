POPULAR QUESTIONS
Frequently Asked Questions

01 Can I request a Taxif ride online?

Yes, in select regions, you can request a ride online. For the best experience and access to all features, download the Taxif ride app.

02 What determines my ride cost?

Your ride cost depends on distance, ride type, demand, and additional services. The app will show the fare before you confirm your ride.

03 What types of cars are available?

Taxif offers various ride types including budget, premium, XL for groups, and special rides like women-only options.

04 How do I get a ride to or from the airport?

You can book airport transfers in advance using the Taxif app. Just enter your flight details and pick-up/drop-off locations.

05 Can I schedule a ride in advance?

Yes! Schedule a ride in advance for airport transfers, meetings, or any other occasion up to 90 days ahead.

06 Is the Taxif app available in my city?

The Taxif app is available in selected cities. Check the app or website for the latest coverage in your area.

07 What payment methods does Taxif accept?

Taxif accepts credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash (where available). Download the app for complete details on payment options.
